Quick tips:
  • Read carefully the Information and Exemption tabs before starting the analyses
  • The complete dataset is available as a downloadable zip file
  • Risk assessment results can be downloaded as .pdf
  • Please search using filters to limit your search to a an area (HELCOM, OSPAR or both), specific port, year, species name or organism group
  • The search results will be displayed as point observations (i.e. circles) or tables and can be downloaded as .csv or .txt files
  • Click on the circle for more information on the observation. To navigate between different observations at the same location use the arrow on top of the info box
  • After your first search, you can choose the option “Update search” to keep your current search settings and adjust them
  • Afterwards you can use the “Reset filters” to delete all your current search filters
  • For a new search, choose “Update search” and then “Reset filters”
  • Feedback and questions are warmly welcome and can be send to: data@helcom.fi
